Cricket World Cup

The Cricket World Cup takes place once every four years. Last World Cup took place in India in 2023 where Australia were champions. The next World Cup will be held jointly in South Africa, Zimbabwe and Namibia in 2027. You can find more information about previous events in Wikipedia - Cricket World Cup and Britannica - Cricket World Cup
